This course aims to empower Grade 7 students to explore robotics, programming, and problem-solving while fostering a sense of social responsibility and environmental awareness.Course Objectives:* Introduction to Robotics: Students will develop a foundational understanding of robotics and its role in addressing real-world challenges, especially those related to the Sustainable Development Goals.* Lego Mindstorms EV3 Proficiency: Students will become proficient in using the Lego Mindstorms EV3 robotics kit, mastering its components, sensors, and motors.* Programming Skills: Through block-based programming, students will learn to code and control the Lego Mindstorms EV3 robot to execute various tasks and missions related to the SDGs.* Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs): Students will become familiar with the 17 SDGs and understand their significance in addressing global issues such as poverty, hunger, climate change, and more.* Problem-Solving for the SDGs: Students will apply critical thinking and problem-solving skills to design, build, and program robots that address specific challenges aligned with the SDGs.* Teamwork and Collaboration: The course will encourage teamwork as students work together to create robots and solutions that contribute to achieving SDGs.* Creativity and Innovation: Students will be encouraged to think creatively and propose innovative solutions to global challenges within the context of robotics.* Presentation and Advocacy: Students will learn how to document and present their SDG-focused robotics projects, fostering communication skills and advocacy for a sustainable future.Student Outcomes and Relationship to Program Educational Objectives           STUDENT OUTCOMESPROGRAM EDUCATIONAL
